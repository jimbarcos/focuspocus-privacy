# Privacy Policy for Focus Pocus: Background Remover

**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## Overview

Focus Pocus: Background Remover ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection and Usage

### Photos and Images
- **What we access:** The App accesses photos from your device's gallery that you choose to edit
- **How we use it:** All image processing happens locally on your device
- **Storage:** We do not store, upload, or transmit your photos to any server
- **Third-party access:** Your photos are never shared with third parties

### Advertising Data (AdMob)
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data

**How AdMob uses this data:**
- To serve personalized ads
- To measure ad performance
- To prevent fraud

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- User accounts or login credentials
- Location data
- Contacts or calendar data
- Analytics beyond ad performance

## Data Storage
- All image editing happens on your device
- No cloud storage or remote servers are used
- Photos are only saved to your device's gallery when you choose to save them

## Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob** - For displaying ads ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google ML Kit** - For on-device image processing (runs locally, no data sent to Google)

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App is rated for general audiences.

## Your Rights
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
- You can uninstall the App at any time
- All your photos remain on your device under your control
